# Dinner Recipes 🌙 (keep it lighter)

Dinner is where we keep it lighter — especially on low-activity days. Lean toward **protein +
vegetables, smaller starches**, and finish ~3 hours before bed (module 09).

---

## 1. Vegetable soup + boiled eggs
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · comfort · light · ~300 kcal · ~16 g protein
- Any vegetable soup (leek, courgette, carrot, tomato, onion) + 2 boiled eggs on the side. Warm,
  filling, very light. Perfect lazy-evening dinner.

## 2. Pan-seared white fish + sautéed greens
**Tags:** 🫒/🍳 both · feel like fish · ~350 kcal · ~32 g protein
- White fish (hake, cod) seared in 1 tsp olive oil with garlic; spinach or chard wilted alongside;
  lemon. Light and quick.

## 3. Big mixed salad with grilled chicken
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · quick · ~380 kcal · ~35 g protein
- Leftover/grilled chicken sliced over a large salad, 1 tbsp olive oil + vinegar. The classic
  high-volume, low-calorie dinner.

## 4. Spanish tortilla (lighter) + salad
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · comfort · ~400 kcal · ~20 g protein
- Tortilla made with fewer potatoes + more onion/courgette and 1 tbsp oil (not the usual flood), big
  side salad. Comfort food, lightened.

## 5. Zucchini "noodles" with prawns
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · feel like pasta · ~330 kcal · ~28 g protein
- Spiralized/ribboned courgette sautéed briefly, prawns with garlic and chili, cherry tomatoes.
  Pasta-night feeling without the heavy carbs.

## 6. Stuffed peppers with tuna & egg
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · ~360 kcal · ~26 g protein
- Roasted peppers filled with tuna, chopped boiled egg, onion, a little tomato. Colorful and filling.

## 7. Pisto with a fried egg (Spanish ratatouille)
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · comfort · ~350 kcal · ~14 g protein
- Courgette, pepper, onion, tomato stewed (1 tbsp oil), topped with an egg. Big vegetable volume,
  cheap, satisfying.

## 8. Grilled sardines + tomato salad
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · feel like fish · ~360 kcal · ~30 g protein
- Sardines (rich in omega-3) grilled, with a simple tomato-onion salad and a little olive oil.
  Cheap, traditional, very healthy.
