# Breakfast Recipes 🍳

Protein + fiber = full till lunch. Pick by mood.

---

## 1. Tomato & egg toast (Basque classic, upgraded)
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · feel like eggs · quick · ~320 kcal · ~18 g protein
- 1 slice wholegrain bread, ½ ripe tomato (grated), 1–2 eggs, 1 tsp olive oil, pinch of salt,
  optional thin slice of good ham.
- Toast the bread, spread the grated tomato, drizzle a little olive oil. Fry/boil/scramble the egg
  and put it on top. Done in 5 minutes.
- *Lighter:* skip the ham. *More protein:* 2 eggs + a spoon of cottage cheese.

## 2. Yogurt power bowl
**Tags:** 🫒/🍳 both · feel like yogurt · quick · no-cook · ~300 kcal · ~20 g protein
- 200 g plain Greek yogurt, a handful of berries (or chopped apple/pear), 6–8 walnuts, cinnamon,
  optional ½ tsp honey.
- Combine in a bowl. That's it.
- *Sweet-tooth version:* more berries + cinnamon instead of more honey.

## 3. Veggie omelette
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · feel like eggs · ~350 kcal · ~24 g protein
- 3 eggs, chopped peppers, onion, tomato, a little grated cheese, 1 tsp olive oil.
- Soften the veg in a lightly oiled pan, pour over beaten eggs, fold. Big, filling, low-carb.

## 4. Proper porridge (the chewable oats)
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · comfort · ~350 kcal · ~14 g protein
- 40 g whole oats, 200 ml milk (or water + a splash of milk), fruit, a few nuts, cinnamon.
- Simmer the oats 4–5 min, top with fruit and nuts. Far more filling than an oat shake.
- *More protein:* stir in a spoon of Greek yogurt or ½ scoop protein powder after cooking.

## 5. Cottage cheese plate
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · feel like savory · no-cook · ~280 kcal · ~22 g protein
- 200 g cottage cheese, sliced tomato, cucumber, olive oil drizzle, black pepper; or sweet version
  with berries.

## 6. Smart breakfast smoothie (replacement, not add-on)
**Tags:** 🫒/🍳 both · feel like a drink · quick · ~300 kcal · ~25 g protein
- 200 g plain yogurt or 250 ml milk, ½ scoop–1 scoop protein powder, a handful of berries, 1 tbsp
  oats, optional ½ banana.
- Blend. Pair with a boiled egg or piece of fruit so you chew something. Keep it occasional.

## 7. Leftovers breakfast
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · feel like real food · ~350 kcal · ~28 g protein
- Last night's grilled fish or chicken + vegetables, warmed up. Unusual but very filling and
  protein-rich — great on a hard work morning.

## 8. Scrambled eggs with smoked paprika & greens
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · feel like eggs · quick · ~330 kcal · ~22 g protein
- 3 eggs, handful of spinach, pinch of smoked paprika, 1 tsp olive oil. Scramble soft. Add a side
  of tomato.
