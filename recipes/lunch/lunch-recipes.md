# Lunch Recipes 🥗🐟 (the anchor meal)

Lunch is often the biggest meal — especially on a hard work day. Build it: **protein + big
vegetables + a portion of smart carbs (beans/potatoes/rice).**

---

## 1. Grilled fish + big salad + beans (the daily classic)
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · feel like fish · ~500 kcal · ~38 g protein
- A fillet of hake/cod/sea bream (grilled with lemon, garlic, parsley), a large mixed salad
  (lettuce, tomato, cucumber, pepper, onion, 1 tbsp olive oil + vinegar), ½ cup white beans.
- Basque coast + Mediterranean plate in one. Filling and light.

## 2. Marmitako-style tuna & potato stew (lightened)
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · comfort · ~520 kcal · ~35 g protein
- Fresh tuna chunks, potatoes, peppers, onion, tomato, smoked paprika, fish or vegetable stock,
  1 tbsp olive oil (measured!).
- Soften veg in measured oil, add potatoes + paprika + stock, simmer until tender, add tuna at the
  end for 3–4 min. A Basque classic made lighter by controlling the oil and loading the vegetables.

## 3. Chicken & vegetable traybake
**Tags:** 🫒/🍳 both · feel like meat · ~480 kcal · ~40 g protein
- Chicken thighs/breast, peppers, courgette, onion, tomato, garlic, herbs, 1 tbsp olive oil. Roast
  on a tray ~30 min. Make extra for tomorrow.

## 4. Lentil & vegetable stew (lentejas)
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · comfort · cheap · ~450 kcal · ~24 g protein
- Lentils, carrot, onion, pepper, tomato, garlic, smoked paprika, bay leaf. Simmer until soft. Cheap,
  filling, high fiber + protein. Add a little fish or egg on top for extra protein.

## 5. Big tuna & egg salad bowl
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · quick · no-cook · ~400 kcal · ~35 g protein
- Lettuce, tomato, cucumber, pepper, onion, 2 boiled eggs, 1 tin tuna, olives, 1 tbsp olive oil +
  vinegar. The "eat more, weigh less" hero plate from module 04.

## 6. Grilled steak + grilled vegetables
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · feel like meat · ~520 kcal · ~42 g protein
- Lean steak, grilled peppers/aubergine/courgette, side salad, drizzle of olive oil. Keep red meat
  occasional (module 05).

## 7. Chickpea & spinach with cod
**Tags:** 🫒 Mediterranean · comfort · ~470 kcal · ~33 g protein
- Chickpeas, spinach, garlic, smoked paprika, tomato, a piece of cod. Classic Spanish *garbanzos con
  espinacas* + fish for protein.

## 8. Squid/seafood with salad
**Tags:** 🍳 Low-carb · feel like fish · ~380 kcal · ~30 g protein
- Grilled or sautéed squid/prawns with garlic, parsley, lemon, and a big salad. Light but very
  satisfying.
