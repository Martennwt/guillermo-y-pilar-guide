The two `.jpg` files above are real free-licensed photos. The other recipe images (the `.webp` files) are
**AI-generated** photorealistic food images (OpenAI gpt-image-1), made because clean free-licensed photos
for those exact dishes were hard to find; they need no third-party attribution. Any of them can be swapped
for a real free-licensed photo later.

To add or replace: drop a photo in this folder, set `img:"assets/img/recipes/<file>"` on that recipe in
`site/index.html`, and (for real photos) add its credit line here. Machine-readable list: `CREDITS.json`.
