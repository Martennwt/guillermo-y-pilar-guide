# The 45-Day Plan 🗺️ (your road, with little wins)

Forty-five days isn't a "diet you survive." It's enough time to **build the habits** that keep going
long after day 45. We split it into **three stages of 15 days**, each with a clear focus and a
**milestone to celebrate.** You don't do everything at once — you stack one easy win on top of
another.

> The goal of the 45 days isn't a number on the scale. It's to come out the other side with a
> **system that runs on its own** — and yes, lighter and feeling better while you're at it.

---

## 🟢 Stage 1 — Days 1–15: AWARENESS & easy swaps

**Focus:** see clearly, change little. No suffering. We're building the foundation.

- ✅ Do the **WHY exercise** (module 01) and put it on the fridge.
- ✅ **Log what you eat** honestly — even roughly. Awareness first; you can't change what you can't
  see.
- ✅ **Drinks first:** cut sugary drinks and juice → water, coffee, tea, sparkling water.
- ✅ **Measure the oil** in the pan (spoon or spray) instead of pouring.
- ✅ **Half the plate vegetables/salad**, a palm of protein, at lunch and dinner.
- ✅ **Kitchen closes after dinner.** Last food ~3 hours before bed.
- ✅ Win the **shopping trip** (module 11): buy the good basics, don't bring home the chips.

🏁 **Milestone at Day 15 — "First leg done!"** You've built awareness and the easy swaps. Most people
already feel lighter and less bloated. Celebrate it (a non-food reward: new gym shirt, a day out,
something you enjoy).

---

## 🟡 Stage 2 — Days 16–30: STRUCTURE & habits

**Focus:** turn the swaps into a rhythm. Lock in protein and meal timing.

- ✅ **Pick your path** (Mediterranean or low-carb/high-protein) and run with it for the next 2 weeks.
- ✅ **Protein at every meal** — hit roughly your target (calculator). This is the hunger-killer.
- ✅ Settle into your **daily rhythm** (module 09): anchor meal, lighter dinner, gentle eating window.
- ✅ Build your **danger-zone systems** (module 12): carry nuts, pre-decide treats, plan around events.
- ✅ Try **2–3 new recipes** from the recipe section so the plan feels rich, not restrictive.
- ✅ Add a little **movement** beyond work if you can — a walk after dinner is golden.

🏁 **Milestone at Day 30 — "Halfway!"** The habits are becoming automatic. Re-weigh (weekly average),
re-measure if you like, and look back at your Day-1 self. Notice the energy, the sleep, the clothes.
Celebrate again.

---

## 🔵 Stage 3 — Days 31–45: CONSISTENCY & make it yours

**Focus:** make it effortless and bullet-proof — a lifestyle, not a project.

- ✅ **Keep it steady.** No need to get stricter — consistency beats intensity now.
- ✅ **Handle a real event well** on purpose (a meal out, a party) using your systems — proof you can
  live your life *and* stay on track.
- ✅ **Refine what works for you.** Keep the meals and habits you enjoy; drop what felt like a chore.
- ✅ **Adjust the numbers** if the scale stalled (trim ~150 kcal or add a walk) or if you're losing too
  fast (eat a bit more).
- ✅ Plan your **"after day 45"**: which 4–5 habits stay forever? (Almost certainly: drinks, measured
  oil, protein + veg, kitchen-closes, win-the-shop.)

🏁 **Milestone at Day 45 — "You did it!" 🎉** You're not "finished" — you've built a system you can run
for life. Look at the whole journey: weight, energy, mood, how food feels now. This is the real prize.

---

## How to use this with the tracker

In the interactive version, a **progress bar** fills as the days go by, and a **badge** unlocks at
Day 15, 30, and 45. On paper, tick the box for each day and color in the milestone. Little wins, made
visible, keep you going when motivation dips.

> **It won't be a straight line.** Some days you'll be off. That's normal and fine. The plan isn't
> broken by a bad day — only by quitting. Bad day? Next meal, back on the road.
