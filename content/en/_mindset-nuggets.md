# Mindset Nuggets (reusable, woven throughout)

Short, punchy truths to sprinkle across the guide — as pull-quotes, sidebars, or the little line at
the bottom of a page. Keep them warm and a little funny. These are the emotional glue.

> Each nugget has an `id` so the site/print build can drop it anywhere.

---

- **id: system-not-willpower**
  "It's not about willpower. It's about the **system** you have ready the moment temptation shows
  up. Hungry-you makes terrible decisions — so let well-fed-you make them in advance."

- **id: keep-the-grade**
  "The art isn't getting an A on one test. It's **keeping** good grades. Anyone can be 'good' for
  three days. We're playing the long game."

- **id: no-crash-rebound**
  "Losing 3 kg in two weeks, celebrating, then sliding back to old habits isn't winning — it's a
  loop. Slow loss that *stays* beats fast loss that *leaves*."

- **id: awareness-first**
  "At the start, the goal isn't perfection. It's **awareness** — noticing where the calories,
  the sugar, and the slip-ups actually hide. You can't change what you can't see."

- **id: not-a-rabbit**
  "This is not 'eat only green stuff like a rabbit.' 🐇 You keep the food you love. We just get
  smarter about the *how much* and the *how often*."

- **id: cravings-not-hunger**
  "A craving feels like hunger but usually isn't. It's a habit knocking on the door. You're allowed
  to not answer."

- **id: fat-as-fuel**
  "If you only ever feed your body from the outside, it never learns to burn the fuel it's already
  carrying. Teach it to use its own reserves — that's where the magic is."

- **id: hidden-calories**
  "The oil in the pan, the second glass of wine, the 'just a taste' while cooking — calories love
  to hide. Find them and you barely have to suffer."

- **id: budget-the-day**
  "Big dinner tonight with paella, wine, the works? Then a light breakfast and lunch. Budget your
  day like money. Spend where it's worth it."

- **id: strong-why**
  "Without a strong WHY, no amount of discipline survives a bad evening. The why is the engine —
  everything else is just steering."

- **id: progress-not-perfection**
  "One off-plan meal doesn't undo a week, the same way one good salad doesn't fix a year. Zoom out.
  Trends beat moments."

- **id: environment-beats-willpower**
  "If the cupboard is full of chips, willpower loses eventually. If it's full of good options, you
  barely need willpower. Win the shopping trip, win the week."
