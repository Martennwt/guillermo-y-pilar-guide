# Welcome 👋

**You already took the first step.**

You sent a photo of an oat-flour shake and asked a simple question: *"Is this good for losing
weight?"*

That question is exactly why this guide exists — and honestly, it's a **great** question. It means
you're paying attention, you're curious, and you're ready to do something. That's the hardest part,
and you've already done it.

So let's start there, with zero judgment.

> **The short answer about the shake:** it's not "bad" — but it's also not a weight-loss hero.
> It's a lot of calories you *drink* in 30 seconds without chewing, and it often leaves you hungry
> again soon after. We'll explain exactly why in a couple of pages (module 03). Stick with us — it's
> one of those "oh, NOW I get it" moments.

## What this is

A simple, friendly guide to losing weight in a way that **lasts** — built around how *you* actually
live: hard work on the building site, Pilar's wonderful Basque cooking, real meals, real life.

We promise three things:

1. **No rabbit food.** 🐇 You keep the food you love. We just get smarter about *how much* and
   *how often*.
2. **No suffering-as-a-strategy.** A body that works as hard as yours needs real food. The goal is a
   *system*, not starvation.
3. **No overwhelm.** Simple on the surface, with more detail underneath only if you want it. If a
   six-year-old couldn't get the main idea, we wrote it wrong.

## How to use it

- Go through it **one piece at a time.** There's no rush. Each section is short.
- The big sections build on each other: first **why**, then **how it works**, then **what to do**,
  then a **45-day plan** with little wins along the way.
- There's a place to **track** what you eat and how you feel — and a printed version for the fridge.

## One promise to make to yourself

You don't have to be perfect. You just have to keep showing up. We're not chasing a quick "A" on
one test — we're learning to keep good grades for life. Slow and steady wins this one.
